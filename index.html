My Journey

Since I was young, I've dreamed of flying. Not just literally, but in connecting people, cultures, and stories. Growing up across three continents - Portugal, Mexico, and Asia - has shaped who I am today: a young professional passionate about diversity, adventure, and the magic of making every person feel special.

What motivates me isn't just aviation, but the opportunity to be part of people's most important moments - from their first honeymoon trip to coming home after years away. Every flight is a new story, every passenger a new connection.

I believe being a flight attendant means being an ambassador of Portuguese hospitality around the world, bringing our warm "welcome" to every corner of the planet.
